Focus Areas

We welcome articles that explore academic literacies within the African context, with particular focus on:

Writing Center & Language Support:

  • Innovative practices in writing centers and language units
  • Supporting multilingual writers and speakers
  • Tutor training and peer mentoring programs

Teaching & Learning:

  • Effective pedagogies for academic literacies development
  • Integrating academic literacies across disciplines
  • Student engagement and learning strategies
  • HOD Insights and Reports

Language Unit Services:

  • Editing services for academic texts
  • Transcription services for research data
  • Language resources and workshops for students and staff
  • Research on academic language practices

Community Engagement:

  • Insights from academic literacies conferences
  • Community-based learning and outreach initiatives
  • Collaborations between institutions and communities

Submission Guidelines

Before submitting:

  • All articles must be original and previously unpublished
  • Word count: 800-1500 words
  • Include 2-3 relevant images (with permissions)
  • Follow APA 7th edition citation style
  • Include a 100-word author bio

Review process:

  • Initial screening: 3-5 business days
  • Peer review: 2-3 weeks
  • Revision period: 1-2 weeks
  • Publication: Quarterly issues
