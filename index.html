Focus Areas

We welcome articles that explore the dynamic landscape of academic literacies, particularly within the African context. Our focus areas include:

Writing Center & Language Support:

  • Innovative practices in writing centers and language units.
  • Supporting multilingual writers and speakers.
  • Tutor training and peer mentoring programs.

Teaching & Learning:

  • Effective pedagogies for academic literacies development.
  • Integrating academic literacies across disciplines.
  • Student engagement and learning strategies.
  • **HOD insights.**

Language Unit:

  • Sharing editing services for academic texts.
  • Providing transcription services for research data.
  • Developing language resources and workshops for students and staff.
  • Conducting research on academic language practices and support.

Conferences & Community Engagement:

  • Insights from academic literacies conferences.
  • Community-based learning and outreach initiatives.
  • Collaborations between institutions and communities.
